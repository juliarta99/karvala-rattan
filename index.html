About Us

KarVala Rattan is a rattan craft business founded on July 2, 2019, in Leuwilaja Village, Sindangwangi District, Majalengka Regency. The business was initiated by four young individuals: Asep Saepullah, Indra Ginanjar, Dede Suhendra, and Udin Suhadin, who share a passion for art and local potential.
